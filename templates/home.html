What NetMon Does

NetMon provides live network visibility by capturing traffic, summarizing protocols & flows, and raising anomaly/threat indicators with explainable reasoning. Built for practical cybersecurity learning and demo-ready analysis.

Core Capabilities

  • Live Visibility: protocols, flows, packets/sec in real time
  • Anomaly Detection: spikes, abnormal packet behavior, threshold breaches
  • Threat Indicators: suspicious patterns summarized with reasons
  • Discovery: internal/external devices + traffic relationships
  • Actionable Output: clear status, severity, and signals
